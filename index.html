Who I Am

Programming has always felt like a creative act to me. I'm most energized by imagining a finished system from nothing and finding the path to get there. Whether it's a simulation engine, a product from scratch, or a game system nobody has built quite that way before. I build software I'm proud to show.

How I Build

Architecture decisions should make the next feature easier, not harder. I think in systems. Modular, clean, and built for the person who comes after me. I bring that same thinking to the people I mentor, helping them find their own approach rather than inheriting mine.
